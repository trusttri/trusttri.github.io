About my name 임제인 (Jane Im)

Ever since I went to school, I've received a lot of questions about my name because it's not a common Korean name. In the U.S., many people ask if "Jane" is my "real name." Long story short, it's both my Korean name and English name. My parents thought 제인 (Jane) is a good name for both. "제인" is how you would write "Jane" in Korean. For example, "Jane Austin" is "제인 오스틴" in Korean. The pronounciation of "제" roughly matches to "Ja" and "인" roughly matches to "ne."
